## Cross-check references

- Chinese Wikipedia supplied a complete nine-college comparison set. Its files
  were marked `Fair use` or `合理使用` and are not retained.
- English Wikipedia supplied six emblems; the Lee Woo Sing file was identical
  to the Chinese Wikipedia copy.
- Baidu Baike supplied usable comparison images for Chung Chi, S. H. Ho, and
  C. W. Chu. No visible watermark was found; the files are not retained.

Source provenance does not establish a redistribution licence. Production use
still requires a project-level rights decision.

## Normalized outputs

Every production SVG uses a `0 0 64 64` viewBox with four units of safe padding,
preserves the source aspect ratio, and contains no script, embedded raster image,
or external resource reference. `preview.html` shows all nine at one rendered
size for human review.
